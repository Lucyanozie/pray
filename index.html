If You Forget Me

If you forget me, do not look for me,
For I shall already have forgotten you.
If you think it long and mad,
The wind of banners that pass, from day to day
And turn into shadows.

Remember the times that we have spent together,
Even if they are now gone like a withered rose,
With a touch of memory that lingers for a time,
Even if the rain had poured from the skies.

Because I will not miss you,
But if you remember me, I will remain.
For I will always be your past.
It does not matter if you are far away.
